### 🔹 Task 1: Business Sales Analysis Dashboard

For this task, I analyzed sales data to understand key business patterns and overall performance.

🛠 **Tool Used:** Power BI  

📈 **Key Insights:**
- Total Sales: **$2.33M** | Profit: **$292K**  
- Sales increased by ~**47%** compared to the previous year  
- Return rate: **5.81%**  
- Consumer segment contributes the highest share (~50%)  
- Sales show an overall upward trend with some fluctuations  
- Certain product categories contribute more to profit  

💡 This project demonstrates how raw sales data can be transformed into meaningful insights through dashboards.

### 🔹 Task 2: Customer Retention & Churn Analysis Dashboard

Analyzed customer data to identify churn patterns, retention trends, and customer behavior using a churn dataset.

📊 **Overview:**
- Total Customers: **7,043**  
- Churned Customers: **1,869**  
- Churn Rate: **26.54%**  
- Retention Rate: **73.46%**  
- Avg Customer Lifetime: **32.37**  

🔍 **Key Insights:**
- Higher churn in early lifecycle
- Month-to-month contracts drive higher churn  
- Customers with fewer services show higher churn  
- Retention improves with longer tenure  

📈 **Dashboard Highlights:**
- Cohort-based churn analysis  
- Tenure vs churn trends  
- Customer demographics analysis  
- Contract & payment behavior insights  

🛠 **Tools & Skills:**  
Power BI | DAX | Data Modeling | Cohort Analysis

### 🔹 Task 3: Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

Built a dashboard to analyze marketing funnel performance and identify conversion drop-offs.

📊 **Overview:**
- Total Visitors: **822K**  
- Total Leads: **244K**  
- Total Customers: **67K**  
- Total Spend: **1.19M**  
- Conversion Rate: **8.1%**  

🔍 **Key Insights:**
- Major drop-off at top of funnel (Visitors → Leads ~70%)  
- Strong mid-funnel conversion  
- Drop observed before final conversion  
- Channel performance varies in cost and efficiency  
- Some channels bring more customers, others are more cost-effective  

📈 **Dashboard Highlights:**
- Funnel visualization  
- Conversion & drop-off analysis  
- Channel performance comparison  
- Cost per customer insights  
- Spend vs customer trends  

🛠 **Tools & Skills:**  
Power BI | DAX | Data Modeling | Funnel Analysis
